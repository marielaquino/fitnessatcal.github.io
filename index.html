FAQ
How do I join?

We recruit on a rolling basis throughout the academic year. Feel free to reach out to us through Facebook or email at fitness.at.cal@gmail.com. A member of our leadership team will reach out to you with the next steps.

How much time do I have to commit?

You get as much as you put in the club! Although all our group training sessions are voluntary and flexible, we believe that the best way to push and motivate each other is to participate in our weekly events.

How is the club structured?

We are an informal, casual group. There are no dues, no fees, and no required commitments. Often new members join to learn about weightlifting and stay because of the community.

What does training look like?

Existing members train on varying schedules. While some hit the RSF at 6:00AM, others lift between 10:00PM â€“ 12:00PM. At any moment, a Cal Fitness member is likely working out so feel free to let us know your schedule. Doing so will allow us to pair you with the right group.

I've never trained before. Is it safe?

Our members range from first time lifters to competitive bodybuilders. We welcome all skill levels. The only thing we ask is for members to be ready to work and have fun!
